Introduction

Bitcoin Cash has always been about empowering everyday people with real peer‑to‑peer money. But for BCH to grow, it needs more than belief — it needs builders, tools, and infrastructure that make it possible for regular people to create on‑chain experiences without friction.

MintCore and CashMint Studio were created to fill that gap. MintCore is a clean, developer‑friendly SDK for CashTokens. CashMint Studio is a creator‑first platform that makes token minting accessible to non‑developers. Together, they form a practical, builder‑focused stack designed to strengthen the BCH ecosystem from the ground up.

About the Project

MintCore and CashMint Studio were created to make building on Bitcoin Cash accessible to everyone — developers, creators, and everyday users. CashTokens opened the door to a new era of on‑chain creativity, but the ecosystem lacked the practical, well‑documented tools needed to turn ideas into real applications.

MintCore (Open Source) gives developers a clean, predictable SDK for working with CashTokens.
CashMint Studio (Closed Source) gives creators a simple, intuitive platform for minting and managing tokens without writing code.

Both tools are built from the perspective of someone who had to learn everything the hard way — and wants to make the path easier for the next person.

Why This Matters for Bitcoin Cash

Bitcoin Cash grows when regular people can build on it. Right now, too many ideas never become real because the tools are too complex, the documentation is too thin, and the learning curve is too steep.

MintCore and CashMint Studio aim to change that by providing:

  • A stable, beginner‑friendly SDK
  • Clear documentation and real examples
  • A creator‑first minting platform
  • Infrastructure that lowers the barrier to entry
  • Tools that help BCH attract new builders and new ideas

This isn’t about hype. It’s about strengthening the foundation of BCH so the next generation of apps, games, businesses, and communities can be built on solid ground.
